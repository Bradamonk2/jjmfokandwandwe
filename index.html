Biography

A Story Forged in
Hardship & Song

JJ Mfoka Ndwandwe, born Zakhele Nxumalo, is a rising Maskandi artist hailing from Nongoma, KwaZulu-Natal, currently based in Amahlongwa. His music carries the red earth and open skies of rural Zululand.

JJ endured a difficult childhood marked by hardship and abuse, experiences that left deep marks on his formative years and interrupted his education. Today, he balances the life of a cattle herder with his calling as a musician — a duality that gives his art its grounded, authentic power.

Inspired by the legendary Mfaz'omnyama, JJ began his musical journey at the age of 14. His artistry is rooted in the oral traditions of Zulu music, channelled through contemporary Maskandi expression.

Music found me when everything else was taken away. It was the one thing that was truly mine — a voice that no hardship could silence.

— JJ Mfoka Ndwandwe
